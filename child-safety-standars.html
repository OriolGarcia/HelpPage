Child Safety Standards

Overview

All content and activity within the Sminos application are closely monitored by the application administrator to ensure the safety and well-being of all users, especially minors. Any misuse of the application may result in account suspension or removal.

Reporting Misuse

Users can report inappropriate behavior or potential child safety concerns directly through the in-app chat feature. Our team is committed to addressing all reports promptly and taking necessary actions to protect our users.

Accountability

The administrator of the Sminos application reserves the right to take appropriate measures, including the removal of users who fail to adhere to the community guidelines or misuse the platform in any way.

Commitment

Sminos complies with all relevant child safety laws and regulations. We collaborate with local and international authorities when necessary to ensure the safety of our platform and its users.
